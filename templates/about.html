About this data set

The original data set was found on kaggle at www.kaggle.com/currie32/crimes-in-chicago. The data includes information on all crimes committed and recorded by the City of Chicago from 2001 to the present date minus the most recent seven days. The data includes detailed information about each incidient including case number, various levels of classification of the crime, locations (ranging from coordinates to region of city), and information on when the crime was committed.
